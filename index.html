About Me

Hello!😁 I am a Postdoctor Researcher in the School of Astronomy and Space Science at Nanjing University.

My previous works include X-ray spectral variabilities in blazars, warm absorber outflows, ambient circumnuclear medium around supermassive black hole, and radio activities of AGN from accretion disk scale to the large-scale environment.

Now my interests focus on AGN feedback on host galaxies and surrounding environments.

You can find my resume here and my research experience here.

Life

My Hometown

I was born in Ningwu (宁武) county in northern Shanxi Province, China, a place with incredibly beautiful scenery

The Fen River , which originates from my hometown, flows through the entire Shanxi Province and eventually empties into the Yellow River, serving as its second-largest tributary. Shanxi Province is one of the birthplaces of the Chinese nation, possessing the largest number of ancient architectural structures in China as well as a long-standing history and culture.
