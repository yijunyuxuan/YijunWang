About Me

Hello! I am a Postdoctor in the School of Astronomy and Space Science at Nanjing University.

My previous works include X-ray spectral variabilities in blazars, warm absorber outflows, ambient circumnuclear medium around supermassive black hole, and radio activites of AGN from accretion disk scale to the large-scale enviroment.

Now my interests focus on AGN feedback on the host galaxies and surrounding environments.

Experience

Postdoctor @ Nanjing University (2021-Present)
